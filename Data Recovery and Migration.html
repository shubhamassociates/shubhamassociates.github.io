Tally Data Recovery & Migration — Full Service

Comprehensive — Secure — Audit-ready

At Shubham Associates we handle Tally ERP data recovery and migration with a strict protocol: preserving original voucher integrity, verifying balances, and delivering import-ready files for your destination system. Our process is built for minimal downtime and maximal compliance.

How we approach it (overview):

  • Initial forensic scan to map recoverable Tally files and check file health.
  • Isolated recovery on secure workstations to prevent further corruption.
  • Reconstruction of ledgers, vouchers, and masters with full timestamp trace.
  • Validation & reconciliation against backup/statement data.
  • Migration scripts to move data to cloud ERP or updated Tally versions, with trial import.
  • Post-migration verification, report, and optional training session for staff.

Why choose this service?

  • Fast triage — we provide the recovery estimate within hours of initial analysis.
  • Secure chain of custody for sensitive financial files.
  • Audit-ready logs, detailed reconciliation, and human-reviewed fixes.

Use-cases: accidental deletion, corrupted ERP files after crash, failed upgrades, ransomware/formatting incidents, hard-disk failure, and migration to newer accounts platforms.
