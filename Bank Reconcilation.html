What is Bank Reconciliation?

1. Transaction Matching

👉 We compare ledger entries with bank statements to detect errors, omissions, or double entries.

  • Deposit & Withdrawal Records
  • Cheque Clearance
  • Bank Charges & Interest

2. Error Detection & Adjustment

👉 Identify mismatches and record adjustment entries to reconcile balances properly.

  • Identify Missing Entries
  • Pass Correcting Entries
  • Update Final Balances

✅ In short:

  • Match Transactions = Accuracy in Accounting.
  • Reconciliation Reports = Transparency for Audit.
