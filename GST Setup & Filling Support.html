What is GST Setup & Filing Support?

1. GST Setup

👉 GST Setup means registering your business under GST and enabling all GST-related features in Tally or any accounting software.

It includes:

  • GST Registration
  • Enabling GST in Tally
  • Setting GST Rates & HSN/SAC Codes
  • Configuring State & Nature of Business

    • ➡️ After this, your company is ready to generate GST-compliant invoices and maintain GST records.

    2. GST Filing Support

    👉 GST Filing means submitting GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc.) to the Government on a monthly/quarterly basis.

    This service covers:

  • Monthly/Quarterly Return Filing
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management
  • GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9 Filing
  • GST Reconciliation
  • Avoiding Late Fees & Penalties

    • ➡️ Basically, we help you stay GST-compliant with zero hassle.

    ✅ In short:

  • GST Setup = Registering & Enabling GST for your business.
  • GST Filing Support = Managing & filing all GST returns on time.
