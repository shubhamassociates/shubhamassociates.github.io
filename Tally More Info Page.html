What is Company Creation in Tally?

Company Creation means registering a company's basic identity in Tally so you can maintain its accounts, GST, payroll, inventory and reports.

1
Enter Company Name, Mailing Name & Address, Country & State (for GST), Financial Year & Books beginning.
2
Set up Chart of Accounts, Voucher Types, Numbering, and Opening Balances for smooth accounting.
3
Configure Inventory, GST, and Payroll modules according to your business needs.
4
Activate security, user roles and backup policies to protect your data.

In short — creation = give identity; configuration = decide how your company will operate inside Tally (features ON/OFF, tax rules, vouchers).
