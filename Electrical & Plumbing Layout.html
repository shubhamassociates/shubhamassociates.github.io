Electrical & Plumbing Layouts — Theory & Key Points

What is Electrical Layout?

An Electrical Layout is the systematic plan showing exact locations and routing for all electrical components — switches, sockets, luminaries, distribution boards and earthing. These plans ensure safety, efficiency and future-proofing.

1
Load Distribution: Properly calculated circuits and MCB sizing to prevent overloads.
2
Concealed Routing: Wiring pathways with ducting and conduit plans to match finishes.
3
Safety: Earthing, isolation switches and placement for easy maintenance.

Quick Facts

Ideal for: Homes, Offices, Retail, Industrial fitouts
Deliverables: CAD drawings, Single line diagrams, BOQ, Site notes
Timeline: Typically 3–10 days depending on scope
Plumbing Layout — What we cover

Plumbing Layout Overview

Detailed routing for supply & drainage including trap positions, venting, pump locations and tank provision. Designs consider maintenance access & water efficiency.

1
Drain Gradients: Correct slopes for waste to avoid blockages.
2
Supply Networks: Separate cold/hot lines with insulation & accessible valves.
3
Water Safety: Backflow prevention & tank venting designs.

Deliverables

Plan types: Layout Plan, Isometric Riser, Schematic
Optional: Hydraulic calculations, Pump schedules
Formats: DWG, PDF, IFC on request
