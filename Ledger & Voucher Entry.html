What is Ledger & Voucher Entry?

1. Ledger Creation

👉 Ledger is the base of every account. We create ledgers for customers, suppliers, banks, and expenses to ensure accurate record-keeping.

It includes:

  • Customer & Supplier Ledgers
  • Bank & Cash Accounts
  • Expense & Income Heads
  • GST & Tax Ledgers

➡️ This ensures proper tracking and structured financial reporting.

2. Voucher Entry

👉 Voucher entries are the backbone of accounting transactions such as sales, purchases, payments, and receipts.

This service covers:

  • Sales & Purchase Vouchers
  • Payment & Receipt Vouchers
  • Contra & Journal Entries
  • Debit/Credit Notes

➡️ This ensures transparent and error-free accounts.

✅ In short:

  • Ledger Creation = Defining structured accounts for business.
  • Voucher Entry = Recording day-to-day transactions accurately.
