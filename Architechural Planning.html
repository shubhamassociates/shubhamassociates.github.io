About Our Architectural Planning Services

Shubham Associates delivers end‑to‑end architectural planning—from feasibility and site zoning to sanctioned drawings and build‑stage documentation. Our approach prioritizes climate, codes and circulation, with robust layer standards, dimension accuracy and consultant‑friendly deliverables (DWG/DXF/PDF).

Master Plan & Zoning

Site analysis, setbacks, FAR/FSI utilization, road hierarchy and amenities placement.

Space Planning & Floor Plans

Human‑centric layouts with furniture blocks, egress and barrier‑free circulation.

Elevations & Sections

Facade articulation, material callouts, levels and structural coordination.

Sanction Drawings & Bylaws

Drawings aligned to local authority formats, scales, title blocks and notes.

MEP & Consultant Coordination

Layer standards for electrical, plumbing & HVAC overlays to reduce site clashes.

Working Drawings & BOQ

Detailing, dimensions, schedules and quantity take‑offs for tendering.

Deliverables include layered DWG/DXF, stamped PDFs, sheet sets and revision logs. We can follow your title block, layer conventions and north‑point styles to integrate seamlessly with contractors.
