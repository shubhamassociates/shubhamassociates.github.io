What is Company Creation in Tally?

1. Company Creation in Tally

👉 Company Creation means setting up a new company in Tally so that you can maintain its accounts, GST, payroll, inventory, etc.

When you create a company in Tally, you basically enter the basic information of your business, like:

  • Company Name.
  • Mailing Name & Address.
  • Country & State (for GST / VAT).
  • Financial Year & Books Beginning From, etc.

    • ➡️ After this step, your company is registered inside Tally, and you can start accounting for it.

    2. Company Configuration in Tally

    👉 Company Configuration means setting rules, features, and options for how the company will work inside Tally.

    This is where you customize your company for your business needs, like:

  • Accounts Configuration
  • Inventory Configuration
  • GST Configuration
  • Payroll Configuration
  • Security Control
  • Voucher Types & Numbering

    • ➡️ Basically, configuration = deciding which features should be ON/OFF for your company in Tally.

    ✅ In short:

  • Company Creation = Giving basic identity to your business in Tally.
  • Company Configuration = Deciding how the company will operate (rules, features, taxation, etc.).
