Company Creation & Configuration — Full Service

Comprehensive — Efficient — Compliance-ready

At Shubham Associates we provide end-to-end company creation and configuration services, handling everything from business registration to software setup. Our process ensures your business is legally compliant and operationally ready from day one.

How we approach it (overview):

  • Initial consultation to understand your business structure and requirements.
  • Document preparation and submission for business registration (Private Ltd, LLP, Proprietorship).
  • GST registration and compliance setup with proper categorization.
  • Company bank account facilitation with partner financial institutions.
  • Tally ERP configuration with chart of accounts, taxation settings, and reporting structure.
  • Staff training on compliance requirements and software usage.

Why choose this service?

  • Streamlined process — we handle all government interactions and paperwork.
  • Expert guidance on optimal business structure for your specific needs.
  • Proper configuration from the start prevents compliance issues later.

Use-cases: New business startups, business expansion, restructuring existing entities, partnership formations, and compliance overhaul for existing businesses.
